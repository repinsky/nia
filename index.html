What Is Network Inventory?
Techopedia defines network inventory management as the process of keeping records of all the IT or network assets that make up a network. In the past, the average network inventory was fairly small and static.
But due to the accelerating rate of technological change, companies today often acquire and decommission IT and network assets so frequently that they sometimes lose track of the state of their network inventory.
